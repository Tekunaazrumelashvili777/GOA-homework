Welcome to Instagram!

Instagram is a social media platform that was launched in 2010 and acquired by Meta in 2012. It allows users to share photos, videos, and other visual content. The app is popular among young people and is used for both personal and commercial purposes. Instagram allows users to share everyday moments, go live, upload short videos (Reels), interact with others, and discover interesting content on the Explore page. The platform is constantly evolving and has become one of the main channels for modern digital communication and marketing.
